About Us

We are a family business motivated by publicizing our culture. We want to next to us you can visit our Chilean vineyards in a familiar and reliable environment.

Our mission in bringing the wine culture closer together in a fun, safe and familiar way all over the world.

Our vision is to position ourselves as the best tourist company in Chile and be the most reliable, friendly and safe.

The Legend of Wine

Concha y Toro Wineyard

Let's enjoy together a walk through the gardens, the park and outside of the summer residence of the Concha y toro family towards the end of the 19th century. You will learn where the Concha y Toro wine comes from in the Variety Garden, place with 26 vine grape vines, we will also visit the old Pírque vineyard and the panoramic view of the Maipo Valley. We will taste wines and visit the cellars of guard, among them, the centennial Casillero del Diablo, place that gave origin to the Legend of the Wine.

Centennial career

Undurraga Vineyard

Let's contemplate together a visit to the park of the Undurraga family as well as a stop in the rincon Aliwen, during which the worldview of the Mapuche, the main indigenous people, is made known From Chile. In the rincon Aliwen, the vegetation of southern Chile was recreated and exhibited high wooden figures typical of the Mapuche culture. We will continue with a tour of vineyards located in the renowned Maipo wine valley, we will pass through the vinification plant and finish offering a trip back in time with a walk through the centenary underground cellars of the Santa Ana de Talagante estate. Tasting of 3 quality reserve wines, in addition each visitor will have a gift glass cup. There is a possibility of acquiring the delicious wines and sparkling wine of the portfolio of brands of Undurraga and a series of entertaining souvenirs in store of the Santa Ana de Talagante estate.

Family history

Cousiño Macul Vineyard

Join us for a beautiful journey through the history of the Cousino Macul vineyard through different places where the wine was processed at the beginning of the 19th century. Together we will visit the vineyards, the centenary winery, the museum and the current winery where our icon wine, LOTA, comes to life. Tasting of 4 wines, 2 White Wines and 2 Grand Reserve Wines, led by a Sommelier. Each visitor will have a gift glass cup.

REcreate, REinvent and Reveal

Bodegas RE Vineyard

Join us for a spectacular tour through a sector of the vineyards, together We will visit the balsamer, the artisan liquor room and the room with jars and barrels. Tasting of 3 wines, RE Pinotel, RE Chardonnoir and RE Cabergnan. It also includes bread board, olives, and the exquisite Olive Oil and Balsam of Wine.

Volcanic origins

Casas del Bosque Vineyard

Join us to one of the most important vineyards in Casablanca; which has gained great prestige, and in 2010 became the best wine to visit in Chile. We will make a beautiful tour of the vineyards and the barrel room. Tasting of 4 wines, 2 reserve wines, 1 large reserve wine and 1 small wine production.

Organic and biodynamic

Emiliana Organic Wineyard

The tour begins with a tour of our gardens where the visitor can share with our animals, those who contribute to the biodiversity that sustains our field. Then a visit to the vineyard where soil types, planted varieties and climatic characteristics of the Casablanca Valley are explained. The tour concludes inside our underground cellar, where we store the biodynamic preparations, responsible for the nutrition and protection of the vines. Tasting of organic wines (strain according to availability), Novas, Signs of Origin and Coyam. We also offer you the possibility to learn to pairing our wines with a selection of organic cheeses or chocolates. Cheese varieties such as Mantecoso Cheese, Cheese with basil, Cheese with cinnamon pepper and Cheese with merquen. And Chocolates of Golden berries, LemonGrass, Salt of Cusco and Aji.

The spirit of the Chilean poet

Nerudian experience

Everything will begin with a visit to Pablo Neruda's house in Isla Negra. Then a few steps away                  you will enjoy an exquisite lunch-meridaje, with a Criolla selection of high level, here you can enjoy the most beloved dish                of the poet, the unique "Caldillo de Congrio" with its poetic recipe. These dishes are joined by the best wines of the Pacific coast, especially aromatic white wines and reds from the cold climate.

Surprise your senses

Vinocratas experience

It is the perfect excuse and starting point to experience the rites of sensory analysis.                  It consists of a 5-course pairing dinner with labels specially selected for the occasion.

As shared in the bar

Barra brava experience

From the bar, a sommelier explains the properties of their labels, through an entertaining tasting blind. So you can be a wine critic for a day.

Valleys and ancestral vines

Heritage Strains Experience

You will taste wines representing valleys such as Huasco, Elqui and Limarí, Itata and Bio-Bio, Maule, Curicó and Colchagua. The Maridaje is made with national dishes, highlighting the qualities of each ingredient and its respective harmony with each bottle chosen and its place of origin.

Oenological milestones of our country

Valles de Chile Experience

Cup after cup you will know the qualities of the main emblematic valleys and strains of Chile. Accompanied by a dinner that combines criollos dishes and other international dishes. Expanding the experience of good drinking with the life culture and the unique landscapes of Chile as a backdrop.
